Biography of Colin O’Neil

About

Colin O’Neil is a student with a strong interest in engineering, programming, and problem-solving. He has experience in software development, mechanical work, and leadership roles. As Car Club Leader, he educates peers on automobile maintenance and mechanics. He also worked as a dockhand at North Lake Tahoe Boat Rental, gaining customer service and technical experience. In the fall, he will attend Santa Clara University (SCU) to study engineering and technology.
